  - <span data-ttu-id="ce32d-113">Se ne zaračuna</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ce32d-113">Non-chargeable</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ce32d-114">Komponente, ki se zaračunajo, je mogoče določiti za opravila, vloge in kategorije transakcij.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ce32d-114">Chargeable components can be defined on tasks, roles, and transaction categories.</span></span>
