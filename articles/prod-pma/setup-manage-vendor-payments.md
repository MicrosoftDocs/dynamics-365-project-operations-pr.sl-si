<span data-ttu-id="f922a-104">Ko dobavitelju odobrite, da dela kot podizvajalec, boste morda želeli zadržati plačilo dobavitelju, dokler vam stranka ne plača za projekt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f922a-104">When you approve a vendor to work as a subcontractor, you might want to withhold payment to the vendor until your customer pays you for the project.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f922a-105">V tem primeru lahko nastavite pogoje za plačilo, ki se izvede po prejemu plačila (PWP), ko skupaj z dobaviteljem nastavite naročilnico (PO).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f922a-105">To support this scenario, you can set up pay-when-paid (PWP) terms when you set up the purchase order (PO) with the vendor.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f922a-111">Vaša organizacija se strinja, da bo stranki zagotovila 100 računalnikov z nameščeno programsko opremo po ceni 150,00 ameriških dolarjev (USD) na računalnik.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f922a-111">Your organization agrees to provide a customer with 100 computers that have software installed, for a price of 150.00 US dollars (USD) per computer.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f922a-112">Nato najamete dobavitelja, ki vam priskrbi računalnike z nameščeno programsko opremo.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f922a-112">You then hire a vendor to provide you with the computers that have software installed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f922a-113">V skladu z dogovorom mora stranka odobriti kakovost računalnikov, preden plača vaši organizaciji.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f922a-113">According to the agreement, the customer must approve the quality of the computers before it pays your organization.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f922a-114">Pravilnik vaše organizacije je, da se plačilo dobavitelju ne izplača, dokler od stranke ne prejmete plačila.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f922a-114">Your organization's policy is to withhold payment to vendors until the customer has paid you.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f922a-115">Zato projekt nastavite tako, da je odstotek plačilo, ki se izvede po prejemu plačila, 100-odstotni.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f922a-115">Therefore, you set up the project so that it has a PWP percentage of 100 percent.</span></span>
