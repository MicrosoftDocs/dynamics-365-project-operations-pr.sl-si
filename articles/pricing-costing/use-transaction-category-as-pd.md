## <a name="set-up-the-transaction-category-field-as-a-pricing-dimension"></a><span data-ttu-id="6269c-168">Nastavitev polja kategorije transakcije kot cenovne razsežnosti</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6269c-168">Set up the Transaction Category field as a pricing dimension</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="6269c-169">Odprite **Nastavitve** > **Parametri**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6269c-169">Go to **Settings** > **Parameters**.</span></span> 
2. <span data-ttu-id="6269c-170">Na zavihku **Cenovne razsežnosti na podlagi zneska** na strani **Parametri** preverite, ali mreža na zavihku prikazuje zapise v entiteti **Cenovne razsežnosti**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6269c-170">On the **Parameters** page, on the **Amount-Based Pricing Dimensions** tab, verify that the grid shows the records in the **Pricing Dimensions** entity.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="6269c-171">Dodajte možnost **Kategorija transakcije** na ta seznam in nastavite polji **Mogoče uporabiti za ceno** in **Mogoče uporabiti za prodajo** na **Da**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6269c-171">Add **Transaction Category** to this list and set the **Applicable to Cost** and **Applicable to Sale** fields to **Yes**.</span></span>
