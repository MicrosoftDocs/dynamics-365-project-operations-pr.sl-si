## <a name="set-up-the-transaction-category-field-as-a-pricing-dimension"></a><span data-ttu-id="2a28f-168">Nastavitev polja kategorije transakcije kot cenovne razsežnosti</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a28f-168">Set up the Transaction Category field as a pricing dimension</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="2a28f-169">Odprite **Nastavitve** > **Parametri**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a28f-169">Go to **Settings** > **Parameters**.</span></span> 
2. <span data-ttu-id="2a28f-170">Na zavihku **Cenovne razsežnosti na podlagi zneska** na strani **Parametri** preverite, ali mreža na zavihku prikazuje zapise v entiteti **Cenovne razsežnosti**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a28f-170">On the **Parameters** page, on the **Amount-Based Pricing Dimensions** tab, verify that the grid shows the records in the **Pricing Dimensions** entity.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="2a28f-171">Dodajte možnost **Kategorija transakcije** na ta seznam in nastavite polji **Mogoče uporabiti za ceno** in **Mogoče uporabiti za prodajo** na **Da**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a28f-171">Add **Transaction Category** to this list and set the **Applicable to Cost** and **Applicable to Sale** fields to **Yes**.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="2a28f-172">V polju **Vrsta razsežnosti** izberite **Na podlagi zneska** in nato izberite prioriteto za možnost **Kategorija transakcije**, saj je povezana s ceno in prodajo.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2a28f-172">In the **Dimension Type** field, select **Amount-based**, and then select the priority for **Transaction Category** as it relates to cost and sales.</span></span>
